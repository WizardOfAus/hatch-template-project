Tell me more

The smart contract has three use cases: farmers, angels and the oracle.

Farmers: can decide to put his land for 'rent' via a smart contract. In return for taking care of the land and returning its soil fertility by either active management or not overusing the land, the farmer can receive either an immediate payment or a form of delayed payment in return for giving some future yield.

Angels: are caretakers who can stake a claim on a certain patch of land via an immediate or recurring payment. In return for their immediate payment, they can make some future claim of gain when the land's soils have improved. After a certain amount of time and a certain amount of features they have staked for (e.g. use of biofuels or microbes) they can either reclaim their original stake plus a small gain or a dividend payment akin to a bond.

The role of the oracle is to determine whether the objectives of each actor's obligations have been fulfilled. In return they can claim a small gas fee.
